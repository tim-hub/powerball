# Personal Style Guidelines


## Sentence Rhythm

- Keep sentences concise and punchy, avoiding unnecessary filler.
- Prefer short paragraphs over long blocks of text.

## Vocabulary

- Use simple vocabulary that is accessible to a broad audience.


## Emoji Usage

- Use emojis often, such as 😊 🤔 😬 🤣 ✅ ❌ 🤞 🙏.
- Place emojis at the end of sentences or as visual markers, not mid-sentence.

## Platform-Specific Rules

- LinkedIn: more professional tone, longer posts OK
- Twitter/X or BlueSky: punchy, max 280 chars, hashtags sparingly
- Email: subject line tips, sign-off style

## What to Avoid

- Avoid corporate jargon ("synergy", "leverage", "circle back").
- Avoid private information or sensitive data.
